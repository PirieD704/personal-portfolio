SpaceBalls: Arena Game
SpaceBalls: A Multiplayer Arena Game
Built with:
SocketIO, Javascript, Angular, NodeJS, PhaserJS
Spaceballs is a multiplayer capture-the-flag arena game. There are two teams and you can have up to four players in a 2 v. 2 match up. The rounds are timed and whoever has possession at the end of two minutes is the winner. Watch out! The flag is not easy to catch!
